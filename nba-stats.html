National Basketball Association (NBA)

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a professional basketball league in North America.
The league is composed of 30 teams (29 in the United States and 1 in Canada) and is
one of the four major professional sports leagues in the United States and Canada.
It is the premier men's professional basketball league in the world.
